# Grill Me

Interview the user about every important branch of the plan until there is a shared understanding. Ask one question at a time. For each question, include your recommended answer and the reason for it.

Use `request_user_input` when the question naturally has two or three concrete options. If a question can be answered by exploring the repo or reading docs, do that instead of asking.

Stop when the remaining uncertainty is low enough to act, then summarize the resolved decisions and unresolved risks.
